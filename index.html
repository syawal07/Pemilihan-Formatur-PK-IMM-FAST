Features

Dashboard

The Carbon Exchange app dashboard includes features like portfolio information, performance graphs, transaction history, carbon project monitoring, market news, data analysis, management accounts, notifications, calculation tools, and customer support for helps users in trading and reducing carbon emissions.

Trade

Carbon Exchange's in-app trading feature allows users to buy, sell or exchange carbon credits through orders market, limit orders, and stop orders. They can see list other orders, view price charts, view history transactions, and manage risk with stop loss or take profit. This trading helps users participate in the carbon market more efficiently and effectively.

Tracking

The "tracking" feature in the Carbon Exchange application makes this possible users to monitor and track changes in the portfolio their carbon over time, including transaction history, performance, and the carbon impact of the projects they support.

Community

The "community" feature in the Carbon Exchange app makes this possible users to interact, share information, and collaborate with communities that have similar interests and goals trade and reducing carbon emissions.
